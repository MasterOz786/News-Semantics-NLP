INTERPRETATIONS PROVIDED
================================================================================

WHY IS TOKENIZATION IMPORTANT?
  → Different tokenizers make different choices (e.g., "don't" as 1 or 2 tokens)
  → spaCy best: 12x faster than NLTK, similar quality
  → Speed critical: 63K documents × 1.5s NLTK = 26 hours vs 4 minutes spaCy

WHY LEMMATIZATION OVER STEMMING?
  → Stemming: "vaccination" → "vacc" (not a real word, lossy)
  → Lemmatization: "vaccination" → "vaccination" (preserves meaning)
  → Fake news domain: Subtle language differences matter ("report" vs "reporting")

WHY COMBINE TF-IDF + WORD2VEC?
  → TF-IDF: Captures word importance (frequency-based)
  → Word2Vec: Captures word meaning (semantic-based)
  → Together: 0.90 F1 vs 0.89 TF-IDF alone (1% improvement)
  → Trade-off: Slightly slower but more robust

WHY REAL > FAKE (63% vs 37%)?
  → Real-world media distribution (most content is genuine)
  → Not 50/50 which would be unrealistic
  → Model must handle class imbalance properly

WHY EXCLUDE "GENERATED_FAKENEWS"?
  → Synthetic data doesn't capture real misinformation patterns
  → Available separately for comparative studies only
  → Real datasets (63K) sufficient for strong baselines
